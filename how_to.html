Getting Started

Welcome to PromptKeeper, your private, local workspace for managing AI prompts directly in your browser. This guide helps you master the features.

Why PromptKeeper?

  • Local & Private: Data stays on your device unless you choose to back it up.
  • Browser Integrated: Access your library instantly via the Side Panel.
  • New feature soon: AI-Optimization. Use local Gemini Nano AI to refine your prompts.

Workspaces

Workspaces allow you to organize your prompts by project, client, or topic (e.g., coding, marketing, personal).

Creating a Workspace

Click the + button in the Workspaces list. Enter a name (max 3 words). Use formatting like my_project or Client X.

Context Menu (Right-Click)

Right-click any workspace in the list to access options:

  • Delete Workspace: Removes the workspace container.
ðŸ’¡ Smart Delete: When you delete a workspace, your prompts are NOT deleted. They are moved to "All Prompts" and tagged with the deleted workspace's name. If you recreate the workspace with the same name later, the prompts will automatically return!

Managing Prompts

Write, edit, and organize your prompts with a full-featured code editor.

Versioning

Every time you modify a prompt, PromptKeeper tracks the changes. Use the Rev: X dropdown in the footer to start a new version branch or revert to an older one.

Paste-to-Page

Click the Paste-to-Page button to instantly insert your active prompt into the current web page's text field (great for ChatGPT, Claude, etc.).

Google Drive Backup

Protect your data by syncing with your personal Google Drive.

  • Auto-Backup: Enabled by default, runs every 30 minutes.
  • Manual Backup: Click "Backup Now" in the options panel.
  • Privacy: Files are stored in a private Application Data folder on your Drive, visible only to this extension.

AI Optimization

If your browser supports Gemini Nano, you'll see the AI tools panel.

  • Magic Enhance: Adds structure and clarity.
  • Formalize: Adjusts tone for professional contexts.
  • Clarify: Removes ambiguity.

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Cmd/Ctrl + S: Save current prompt
  • Cmd/Ctrl + B: Open Side Panel
