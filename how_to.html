Visual Cues & Defaults

  • Orange Pulse üü†: If you see an orange pulsing border around the editor, it means you have Unsaved Changes.
  • Auto-Save: Enabled by default. The system saves your work automatically after a short pause in typing or when switching prompts.

üíæ Backup, Export & Portability

Your data belongs to you. There are two ways to move data:

  1. Google Drive Sync (Side Panel): Connect your account to sync an encrypted snapshot to a hidden private folder (`appDataFolder`) in your Drive. Ideal for backup.
  2. JSON Export (Options Page): In the full "Manage" view, look for the Export | Import links in the footer. This creates a raw JSON file of your entire library that you can share or inspect manually.
